Admission Details

How to Apply

Admissions to Uluberia Government Polytechnic are strictly through JEXPO, conducted by the West Bengal State Council of Technical & Vocational Education and Skill Development.

Eligible candidates must register for the JEXPO examination and choose “Uluberia Government Polytechnic” during the counselling phase.

Visit the Official WBSCT&VE&SD Website

Important Dates (tentative)

  • Application opens: January – February
  • Admit cards issued: April
  • JEXPO exam: May (first Sunday)
  • Results & counselling: June – July
  • Class commencement: August

Eligibility

  • Passed Class 10 (Madhyamik) or equivalent with Mathematics, Physical Science & English.
  • Minimum 35 % aggregate (no rounding).
  • Age ≤ 21 years as on 01-07-YYYY (3-year relaxation for SC/ST candidates).
